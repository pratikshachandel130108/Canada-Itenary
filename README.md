# Canada-Itenary
A complete and practical travel itinerary guide for anyone planning a trip to Canada. This project is designed to help travelers explore Canada in a structured, time-efficient, and budget-friendly way.


This project was completed as part of my AI Learning Challenge, where I am actively exploring Generative AI, AI Agents, and AI-powered productivity tools through hands-on project.

Planning a trip to Canada can be overwhelming due to its vast geography, multiple cities, and countless attractions. This itinerary solves that problem by providing a day-by-day travel plan, covering:

-Major tourist attractions

-Local experiences 

-Transportation guidance 

-Budget estimation 

-Time optimization

Whether you're a solo traveler, couple, or group — this itinerary helps you make the most of your Canada trip.

## Travel Tips

-Book flights early for cheaper prices

-Use public transport (metro/bus) in cities 

-Carry valid travel insurance 

-Always check weather before packing 

-Keep some cash + international card 

-Respect local rules and nature zones
